Danghost is an arcade puzzle game, inspired by fighting games, in which you play an exorcist who needs to hoover up small ghosts. Make combos, use unique spells and beat your opponents! Playable in single-player, it's with the local multiplayer mode that the game will reveal all its spice!
With 12 character choices, each with special attacks, and the ability to play up to 4 players locally, make strategic chains and send lethal moves at your friends. Get the highest score and survive to win the game!
This game is intended for PC, Mac, and consoles.
